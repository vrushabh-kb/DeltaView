# 🚀 DeltaView

DeltaView is an AI-powered stock analysis platform designed for Indian equity investors. It combines financial statement analysis, company ranking algorithms, portfolio tracking, and AI-generated insights into a single intuitive dashboard.

## 📌 Overview

Investors often struggle to identify quality businesses, compare companies objectively, and understand what financial metrics actually mean. DeltaView addresses this problem by transforming raw financial data into actionable insights using a proprietary scoring engine called **DeltaScore™**.

The platform helps users:

* Discover fundamentally strong companies
* Compare stocks side-by-side
* Analyze revenue and profit trends
* Track personal portfolios
* Understand the reasoning behind rankings
* Identify investment styles through a personality assessment

---

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 Investor Personality Test

A short assessment that categorizes users as:

* Growth Investor
* Value Investor
* Quality Investor

Based on the result, DeltaView automatically recommends the most suitable investment strategy.

---

### 📊 AI Stock Screener

Ranks companies using the proprietary **DeltaScore™** system.

The score evaluates:

* Revenue Growth (Sales CAGR)
* Profit Growth (Profit CAGR)
* Operating Margin Quality
* Debt Sustainability

Users can instantly identify high-quality businesses based on their preferred investment style.

---

### 🔍 Company Deep Analysis

Each company page provides:

## 📈 DeltaScore™ Methodology

DeltaScore evaluates companies across four core pillars:

| Metric           | Purpose                  |
| ---------------- | ------------------------ |
| Sales CAGR       | Measures business growth |
| Profit CAGR      | Measures earnings growth |
| Operating Margin | Measures profitability   |
| Debt-to-Cashflow | Measures financial risk  |

Different weights are applied depending on the selected strategy:

* Growth Investing
* Value Investing
* Quality Investing

This creates a customized ranking system tailored to investor preferences.
